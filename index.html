Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • PDF Blueprint generation — your birth data is passed to our AI pipeline (Dify + DeepSeek) to generate your personalised energy report.
  • Report delivery — your email address is used to send you the confirmation email and your completed PDF Blueprint.
  • Service improvement — anonymised, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., number of reports generated) may be used to improve the product. No individual is identifiable from this data.
  • Customer support — if you contact us, we use your email to respond.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making that has legal or similarly significant effects.

Section 05

Data Storage & Security

Personal data submitted through the Blueprint form is processed transiently through our AI pipeline and stored only as long as necessary to generate and deliver your PDF report (typically under 30 minutes).

Your email address is retained in our mailing records to enable re-delivery of your PDF upon request and to send you product updates you have opted in to. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 6).

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • API keys stored as environment variables, never in client-side code
  • chrome.storage.local for on-device data (not synced to Google servers)
  • Access to production systems restricted to authorised personnel only

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable precautions proportionate to the sensitivity of the data we handle.

Section 06

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at support@wuweholographic.com. We will respond within 30 days.

To delete your local data immediately: open Chrome → Extensions → Quantum Flow → Remove extension. This erases all locally stored data (email, Key, submission status) from your device.
